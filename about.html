What is Moodle LMS?

A flexible LMS you can shape to your needs

Moodle LMS is a flexible learning management system that lets you design and deliver learning your way. Create courses that feel natural for your educators and intuitive for your learners - all within a platform you can shape to match your approach, your goals and your community.

A flexible foundation. A familiar interface. A platform that grows with you.

Easy integration

Connect Moodle LMS with your existing tools and systems. Integrate video conferencing, authentication, academic integrity tools, content tools, and more. Explore LMS integration

Secure and scalable

Effortlessly scale your Moodle site and protect your data with strong privacy and security controls. Learn how Moodle LMS helps you manage compliance in LMS integration

Accesible for all

Moodle LMS supports inclusive learning with WCAG 2.2 AA compliant design, built-in accesibility check, screen-reader compatibility, and other tools that help you create content for all learners.

AI, your way

Moodle gives you choice and control over how AI is used in your learning environment. Our approach is guided by our AI Principles, which put people, ethics, and transparency first. AI features are designed to support educators, protect data, and keep humans at the centre of teaching and learning.
